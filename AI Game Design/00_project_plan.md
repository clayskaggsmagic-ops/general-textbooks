# AI Game Design — Project Plan

## Objective
Design the perfect drinking game from first principles using systematic research and game design theory.

---

## Phase 1: Research & Design Foundations ✅ COMPLETE

**Deliverable:** `01_research.md`

**What was done:**
- Analyzed 20+ classic and modern drinking games
- Extracted 5 core design laws (Simplicity, Everyone Involved, Escalation, Balanced Drinking, Social Connection)
- Cataloged all mechanic types (input, output, structural)
- Reviewed commercial products and market gaps
- Identified the meta-pattern all great drinking games share
- Established design direction: layered simplicity + escalation + one-page rules

**Key Finding — The Gap:**
No game combines Buzzed-level simplicity with Kings Cup-level escalation, NHIE-level social energy, AND a clear dramatic climax. That's what we're building.

---

## Phase 2: Game Design & Game Book ✅ COMPLETE

**Deliverable:** `02_game_book.md`

**What was created:**
1. **Game name** — **Last Straw** ("because someone's gotta drink that cup")
2. **One-page rules** — "Draw a card. Read it out loud. Do it." Done.
3. **75-card deck** — 9 card types (YOU, TARGET, EVERYONE, CHALLENGE, SOCIAL, DOOM, RULES, BOMB, WILD)
4. **Escalation mechanism** — The Doom Cup fills visibly throughout the game
5. **Climax event** — The Bomb card (buried in the bottom third) ends the game; drawer drinks the entire Doom Cup
6. **Complete game book** — Full rules, sample cards, game arc diagram, variants, competitive positioning, and box contents
