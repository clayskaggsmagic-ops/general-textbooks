# 🔥 LAST STRAW

### *The drinking game that actually has an ending.*

---

## THE PITCH

**Last Straw** is a card game for 3–12 players. Draw a card, do what it says, and try not to be the one holding the deck when the Bomb goes off. Every card is dead simple. The game builds. And someone always gets wrecked at the end.

**You need:** This deck. Drinks. One empty cup in the center of the table (the "Doom Cup").

**Setup:** Shuffle the deck. Place the Doom Cup in the center. Everyone gets a drink. That's it.

---

## HOW TO PLAY

> **On your turn:** Draw a card. Read it out loud. Do what it says. Pass the deck left.

That's the entire game. Every card tells you exactly what to do. No memorizing rules, no looking things up.

But here's the thing — **three special card types change everything:**

---

## THE THREE CARDS THAT MATTER

### 💀 DOOM CARDS (6 in the deck)
When you draw a Doom Card, **pour some of your drink into the Doom Cup** in the center. The cup fills slowly as the game goes on. You can pour as little or as much as you want. Everyone watches it grow.

### 💣 THE BOMB (1 in the deck)
Buried somewhere in the bottom third of the deck. When you draw the Bomb, the game is over — **you drink the entire Doom Cup.** That's the climax. That's the moment everyone's been waiting for. The anticipation builds every time a Doom Card is drawn because the cup gets heavier and you know the Bomb is coming.

### 👑 RULES CARDS (6 in the deck)
When you draw a Rules Card, **you make a new rule that lasts the rest of the game.** Break the rule? Drink. Rules stack — by the end of the game, there are 4–6 active rules and everyone is constantly tripping over them.

> **Examples of rules people create:** "No saying anyone's real name." "You must drink with your non-dominant hand." "Before you drink, you have to make eye contact with someone and wink." "No pointing." "You cannot say the word 'drink.'"

## THE 75-CARD DECK

The rest of the deck is action cards. Every card is a self-contained instruction — no memorization needed. They're organized into five types:

| Symbol | Type | What It Does | Count |
|--------|------|-------------|-------|
| 👆 | **YOU** | The person who drew it drinks | 10 |
| 👉 | **TARGET** | You pick someone — they drink | 10 |
| 🌊 | **EVERYONE** | The whole table drinks | 8 |
| 🎯 | **CHALLENGE** | A quick group challenge — loser(s) drink | 15 |
| 🗣️ | **SOCIAL** | Confessions, votes, and conversations — someone (or everyone) drinks | 15 |
| 💀 | **DOOM** | Pour into the Doom Cup | 6 |
| 👑 | **RULES** | Create a persistent rule | 6 |
| 💣 | **BOMB** | Drink the Doom Cup. Game over. | 1 |
| 🃏 | **WILD** | Drawer's choice: pick any category | 4 |
| | | **TOTAL** | **75** |

## SAMPLE CARDS

### 👆 YOU Cards
- *"You hesitated. Drink."*
- *"Drink once for every person at this table who's taller than you."*
- *"You drew this card. That's honestly reason enough. Drink."*
- *"Drink if you've texted an ex in the last month."*

### 👉 TARGET Cards
- *"Pick the worst tipper at this table. They drink."*
- *"Point at the person most likely to cry at a movie. They drink."*
- *"Pick someone. They drink. They also pick someone. That person also drinks."*
- *"Make eye contact with someone without laughing. First to laugh drinks. If you both laugh? Both drink."*

### 🌊 EVERYONE Cards
- *"Waterfall. Drawer starts drinking. Each player clockwise starts when the person before them starts. You can't stop until the person before you stops."*
- *"Everyone takes a sip. That's it. Sometimes life is simple."*
- *"Everyone points at who they think is the drunkest. That person drinks for every finger pointed at them."*
- *"If you have your phone on the table right now, drink."*

### 🎯 CHALLENGE Cards
- *"Category: Types of cheese. Go around the circle. First person to repeat or hesitate drinks."*
- *"Staring contest with the person across from you. Loser drinks."*
- *"Rhyme time. Drawer says a word. Go around — first person who can't rhyme drinks."*
- *"Everyone holds up their drink. Last person to take a sip drinks double."*
- *"Thumb Master: Silently place your thumb on the table whenever you want for the rest of the game. Last person to notice and copy you each time? They drink."*
- *"Count to 20 as a group. No two people can say the same number at the same time. If two people talk at once, everyone drinks and restart."*

### 🗣️ SOCIAL Cards
- *"Most likely to get arrested. Everyone points on 3. Person with the most fingers drinks that many sips."*
- *"Never have I ever: Drawer says one thing. Everyone who HAS done it drinks."*
- *"Hot Seat: Person to your left has 30 seconds. Everyone asks them rapid-fire yes/no questions. Every 'yes' = a sip."*
- *"Confessional: Share your most embarrassing story from the last year, or drink three times."*
- *"The table votes: Who here is the worst driver? That person drinks."*
- *"Two Truths and a Lie. Tell three things about yourself. The table guesses the lie. If they're wrong, they drink. If they're right, you drink."*

### 💀 DOOM Cards
- *"Pour some of your drink into the Doom Cup. You choose how much. But remember — someone's drinking all of it later."*
- *"DOOM. Add to the cup. Look the person across from you in the eye while you do it."*
- *"The Cup grows. Pour some in. Tell the person to your right something nice while you do. They'll need the kindness later."*

### 👑 RULES Cards
- *"You are now the Rule Maker. Create one rule that applies for the rest of the game. Anyone who breaks it drinks."*
- *"New rule time. Make it weird. Make it funny. Make it unforgettable. It lasts until the Bomb goes off."*

### 🃏 WILD Cards
- *"Dealer's choice. Pick any category: YOU drink, TARGET someone, challenge the group, or add a rule. Your call."*

## THE ARC

This is what makes Last Straw different from every other card drinking game:

```
EARLY GAME (Cards 1-25)
├── Light and fun. Simple sips. People are learning names.
├── First Doom Card hits → curious, small pour
└── First Rule Card hits → "okay this is interesting"

MID GAME (Cards 25-50)
├── The Doom Cup is visibly filling up
├── 2-3 active rules → people keep accidentally breaking them
├── Challenges get competitive, socials get personal
└── Everyone starts counting how many cards are left

LATE GAME (Cards 50-75)
├── The Doom Cup is HEAVY. Everyone can see it.
├── 4-6 active rules → total chaos, constant rule-breaking
├── Every card draw is tense — "is this the Bomb?"
├── The table collectively loses it when another Doom Card appears
└── 💣 BOMB → One person drinks the Doom Cup → SCENE
```

**The Bomb is always in the bottom 25 cards** (shuffle it into the last third). This guarantees at least 50 cards of gameplay before it can appear, which means the Doom Cup has time to fill and the rules have time to stack. But you never know *exactly* when it hits.

## OPTIONAL VARIANTS

**🏆 Redemption Round:** Before the Bomb victim drinks the Doom Cup, they get one chance — a rock-paper-scissors showdown with the person to their left. Win? That person drinks half. Lose? Full cup, no mercy.

**👫 Teams:** Play in pairs. When your partner draws a card, you're affected too. Doom Cup penalty splits between the pair.

**🌶️ Spicy Mode:** Shuffle in 5 blank cards. Write your own cards before the game starts. The group decides intensity.

**🧊 Chill Mode:** Remove the Doom Cup and Bomb entirely. Play as a pure social card game — no penalty cup, just the cards.

## ONE-RULE SUMMARY

> **Draw a card. Read it out loud. Do it. Doom Cards fill the cup. Rules Cards create rules. The Bomb ends the game — whoever draws it drinks the cup.**

That's Last Straw.

---

## WHY THIS GAME EXISTS

| Problem with existing games | How Last Straw solves it |
|---------------------------|------------------------|
| Too many rules to memorize (Kings Cup) | Every card is self-contained — zero memorization |
| No climax or arc (Buzzed, These Cards) | Doom Cup fills visibly; Bomb creates an inevitable dramatic moment |
| People sitting around doing nothing (Beer Pong) | Every card involves multiple players (challenges, votes, group drinks) |
| One person gets too drunk (Ride the Bus) | Drinking is distributed across all card types; Doom Cup is the only concentrated penalty |
| Gets repetitive (Never Have I Ever) | 75 unique cards across 5 types; Rules Cards make every game different |
| Complex setup (Drink-A-Palooza) | Shuffle cards. Place empty cup. Play. |
